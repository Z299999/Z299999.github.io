## Film Projects

I create documentary and creative films exploring themes of nature, identity, and human experience.

### Documentary Shorts

- *Echos* — Director, cinematographer, editor  
  *June 2025 – September 2025 · Yunnan, China*  
  <img src="/assets/img/films/echos/poster.jpg" alt="Echos poster" style="max-width: 320px; width: 100%; height: auto; margin: 0.5rem 0 1rem 0;">  
  An ethnomusicographic film set in the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province, funded by the University of Oxford. Through fieldwork, it explores how sound is intertwined with the land, while reflecting on how economic development, cultural change, and tourism shape these musical traditions.  

- *Current* — Director, producer, editor  
  *June 2024 – July 2024 · Los Angeles, CA, USA*  
  <img src="/assets/img/films/current/poster.jpg" alt="Current poster" style="max-width: 320px; width: 100%; height: auto; margin: 0.5rem 0 1rem 0;">  
  A short documentary developed on behalf of the University of Southern California, following the journey of an Uber driver in Los Angeles. The film highlights multiple perspectives of his life, mirroring the filmmaker’s own experiences. <a href="https://youtu.be/0y3ZnJ1kOK0">Trailer</a>

- *Plant Pears for Your Heirs* — Production manager, cinematographer, editor  
  *December 2021 – February 2022 · Shenzhen, Guangdong, China*  
  <img src="/assets/img/films/plant-pears/poster.jpg" alt="Plant Pears for Your Heirs poster" style="max-width: 320px; width: 100%; height: auto; margin: 0.5rem 0 1rem 0;">  
  A 17-minute award-winning documentary focusing on the lives of construction workers building a university campus. It highlights their often overlooked presence and contributions, and draws attention to the shared space between students and workers, experienced at different times.
